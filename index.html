Who I Am

I'm Sahithi Yerramsetty, a Data Engineer and Analyst who sees data as rhythm, structure, and story. I love designing data experiences that feel alive — transforming scattered datasets into well-orchestrated pipelines that flow as seamlessly as a great song.

My journey started during undergrad when I built an ML model predicting YouTube ad views — seeing a model learn for the first time felt magical. Since then I've worked on ETL optimization, data modeling, and visualization at Deloitte USI serving the Kroger Pharmaceuticals account, processing 400M+ records per day across Azure and Databricks.

I believe great data work blends precision, design, and empathy. Good data systems don't just process — they communicate, empower, and inspire. Currently finishing my MS in IT at ASU (4.0 GPA) and actively seeking Data Engineer and Analytics Engineer roles across the US.
